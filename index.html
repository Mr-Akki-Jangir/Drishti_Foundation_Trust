About Us

Buddh vihar within the sand dunes and foot hill of a small village Jaipahari has been constructed in Shekhawati, which is recognized for struggle of life in desert zone of Rajasthan.

This area is recognized famous for business families who played a remarkable role in providing education to all.Shekhawati is a fertile land for developing new thought process and social order.

Inspired by Ambedkarism and Budhism,a son of soil Prof. Onkar Mal settled in gurgon/Gurugram erminated a thought for building , a Buddh vihar on a land piece donated by his elder brother Mistry sh. Mali ram ,being a Arya samaji

The entire financial input from Prof. Onkar Mal which had been earned through his motivational/educational lectures given by him in various universities and renowned institutes of higher learning.

Sh. Mali Ram BITSIAN provided all technical support in terms of drawings, architecture, beautiful front View of the vihar , front main gate design giving a look for Buddhism ideology.

After leveling of land the foundation stone was laid 0n 14th April 2021 and construction was started. The vihar building was completed in all respect by end of Nov`2022 through the effective leadership and monitoring/supervision and timely funds flow from prof. Onkar Mal .

The idea of forming a trust existed ever earlier in life time of Master Raghuvir Prasad, who was a great social worker in the field of education and community reformative activities KR Buddh prabodhan vihar trust jaipahari was therefore born and registered on 22-07-2021. Buddh vihar was opened and inaugurated by Hon`ble Rajendra Pal Gautam cabinet minister of Delhi NCR region on 5th Dec 2021

The immovable property in the form of vihar building costing Rs 25,60,500/- had been transferred to Trust in the Trustees board meeting held on on 30.11.2022 with unanimous decision. The digital library having literature of big thinkers and computers and TV and other logistics are transferred for public use.
